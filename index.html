Llamas are legendary.
It is said that llamas once ruled the world before humankind. Their gentle, shy, but curious demeanor formed a peaceful society. Nowadays, they live as domesticated livestock.
But Be Careful.
Llamas are seemingly harmless, but their dormant power is beyond our comprehension. One must be respectful when approaching and interacting with a llama.
